Pizza

This pizza recipe is a classic favorite, featuring a crispy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and your choice of toppings. Whether you prefer pepperoni, vegetables, or a simple Margherita, this recipe is perfect for a family dinner or a casual get-together.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, oregano
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  • Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  • Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  • Add your choice of toppings.
  • Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  • Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese if desired, and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
