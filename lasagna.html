Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe delivers layers of comfort and flavor! Featuring sheets of pasta, a rich meat sauce (or your preferred filling, like ricotta), and a generous topping of melted cheese, it's a family favorite perfect for gatherings or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noddles
  • Tomato puree
  • Tomato paste
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  • In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  • Add tomato puree, tomato paste, marinara sauce, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to the skillet. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  • Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and more meat sauce. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  • Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
