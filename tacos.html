Tacos

The recipe for these tacos is very simple, featuring soft or crispy tortillas filled with seasoned meat, fresh vegetables, and your choice of toppings. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a fun gathering with friends, these tacos can be customized to suit any palate.

Ingredients

  • Soft or crispy tortillas
  • Ground beef, chicken, or beans
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole
  • Salsa or pico de gallo
  • Chopped onions and cilantro (optional)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Steps

  • In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground meat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  • Add taco seasoning and a little water to the skillet, stirring to combine. Simmer for about 5 minutes.
  • Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  • Assemble the tacos by placing a portion of the meat mixture on each tortilla.
  • Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, guacamole, salsa, onions, and cilantro as desired.
  • Serve with lime wedges and hot sauce on the side.
