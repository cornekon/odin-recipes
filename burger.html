Burger

The classic burger recipe, and an old time favorite, featuring a juicy beef patty topped with melted cheese, fresh lettuce, tomato, and your choice of condiments. Perfect for grilling season or a casual weeknight dinner, these burgers can be customized with various toppings to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80/20 blend recommended)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheese (American, cheddar, or your choice)
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions (optional)
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (or your favorite condiments)
  • Cooking oil for grilling

Steps

  • Preheat your grill or stovetop skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Form the ground beef into patties, about 1/2 inch thick. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  • Add a little oil to the grill or skillet to prevent sticking.
  • Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on one side, then flip them.
  • Add cheese on top of each patty and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the cheese is melted and the burger is cooked to your desired doneness.
  • Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  • Assemble the burgers by placing the patty on the bottom bun, followed by lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, and your choice of condiments.
  • Top with the other half of the bun and get ready to serve immediately.
